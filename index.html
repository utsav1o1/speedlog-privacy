Privacy Policy for SpeedLog

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

SpeedLog ("we", "our", or "us") built the SpeedLog app as a free app. This page informs users about our policies regarding collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

SpeedLog stores internet speed test results (download speed, upload speed, ping) locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Third Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to serve personalized ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Network Access

The app requires internet access solely to perform speed tests and display advertisements. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

Local Storage

All speed test history is stored locally on your device using SQLite. You can delete all data at any time from the Settings screen.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
